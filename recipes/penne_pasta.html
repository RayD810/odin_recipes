Penne Pasta with Pesto Sauce

Description

Penne Pasta coated in pesto sauce with tomatos, zuchinni, and asparagus - garnished with sliced basil. The perfect date dish when you're crunched for time and ready to eat

  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 lb large chicken breast, cooked and diced
  • 2 cups asparagus, cut into 1 1/2-in 38mm pieces
  • 10 oz cherry tomatos, halved
  • 2/3 cup pesto
  • 2 cups whole wheat penne
  • Sliced basil for a garnish
  1. Heat the oil in a large nonstick skillet. Toss in the asparagus, season with a bit of salt, and sautee until they begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
  2. Pour on the pesto, pasta and combine chicken. Stir until combined
  3. Toss in the cherry tomatos and give everything a stir to combine and warm throughly.
  4. Distribute pasta mixture evenly amongst containers for storage.
  5. Top with basil for garnish.

    6. Mixture can be stored for up to 4 days.

  6. Enjoy!
